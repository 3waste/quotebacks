we've found quotebacks to be really useful for a couple things:

  1. quoting other people at length when you write online (here's an example in a post by Tom).
  2. quoting yourself in your own blog posts- sometimes you've written the perfect thing already. (here's an example in a post by Toby.)

quotebacks can be used anywhere a normal block quote might go. they help give added context, structured metadata, and better linking back to the original author.
